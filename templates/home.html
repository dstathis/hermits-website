About the Club

The Deranged Hermits host regular Legacy and Premodern tournaments at Dragonphoenix Inn in Athens, Greece. We welcome players of all skill levels.

🃏 Proxies Welcome

All proxies must be color prints of the card. Everyone can play their favorite deck!

🏆 100% Prize Pool

The entire entry fee goes to the prize pool. Prizes are paid out as Deranged Hermits credit, redeemable for singles.

📍 Athens, Greece

Events are held at Dragonphoenix Inn. Registration at 16:00, Round 1 at 16:15.
